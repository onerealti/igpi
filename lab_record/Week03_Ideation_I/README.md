## Theory
### SCAMPER Analysis
SCAMPER is an acronym for a schema of brainstorming questions that help teams spark ideas for new products or services by encouraging them to think about how they can modify existing parts:
*   **S**ubstitute: Replace materials, components, or processes.
*   **C**ombine: Merge different parts or functions together.
*   **A**dapt: Adjust or copy ideas from other fields or nature.
*   **M**odify/Magnify: Alter the size, shape, or key parameters.
*   **P**ut to another use: Find alternative functions for components.
*   **E**liminate: Simplify by removing parts or processes.
*   **R**everse/Rearrange: Change the order or layout.

### 2. SCAMPER Analysis
We applied SCAMPER to refine the solar tracker design:
*   **S (Substitute):** Substitute plastic gears with metal gears (e.g., MG995 servos) for higher torque and durability under wind.
*   **C (Combine):** Combine the LDR mount with the panel frame to reduce part count and assembly steps.
*   **A (Adapt):** Adapt the "sunflower" behavior—closing or flattening the panel at night to protect the active surface.
*   **M (Modify/Magnify):** Magnify light sensitivity by using a deeper "well" for each LDR sensor to block ambient lateral scattering.
*   **P (Put to another use):** Use tracking movement logs to analyze peak sunlight hours and identify local shading profiles.
*   **E (Eliminate):** Eliminate the need for continuous 360-degree rotation to simplify wiring (constrain to 180-degree limits).
*   **R (Reverse/Rearrange):** Rearrange the motor placement so the Azimuth motor is at the base (heaviest motor) and the Elevation motor is mounted on the vertical pillar.

### 3. Mind Map Description
Our mind map centered on "Dual-Axis Efficiency."
*   **Mechanical Branch:** Explored gears, servos, thrust bearings, and 3D printing materials.
*   **Electrical Branch:** Analyzed Arduino, LDR sensors, decoupling capacitors, and power source management.
*   **Environmental Branch:** Focused on wind, rain, dust, and UV protection.
*   **User Interface Branch:** Evaluated manual calibration buttons and LCD status displays.
*   *Key Connection:* Linked **Weight Distribution** directly to **Servo Longevity** (leads to counterweight concept).
